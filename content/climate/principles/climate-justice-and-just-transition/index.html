Climate Justice and Just Transition

Prioritize vulnerable communities, equity, livelihood protection, fair finance and support for exposed populations.

Overview

Climate action must be fair

Climate impacts often fall hardest on communities with the least resources to prepare, adapt and recover.

FutureWorld uses this principle to keep equity, participation, livelihoods and local dignity central to climate planning.

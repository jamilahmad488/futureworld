FutureWorld Climate Intelligence Principle

#016 Community-Centered Climate Action

Climate action must be designed with local communities, not only delivered to them.

Future-Ready PrincipleParticipationLocal InstitutionsOwnership
Community action cycleTrust, participation and stewardship
ConsultListen to local risk and needs.
OrganizeBuild local committees and roles.
ImplementWork with community labor and skills.
ProtectLocal stewardship and monitoring.
LearnFeedback and improvement.

Core rule

The strongest climate action combines scientific evidence with community ownership, local knowledge and long-term stewardship.

FutureWorld application

This principle reflects FutureWorld's field roots in community mobilization, VDCs, watershed restoration and assisted natural regeneration.

Editorial checklist

  • Who is the stakeholder community?
  • How are they consulted?
  • What role do they play in implementation?
  • How is local stewardship maintained?

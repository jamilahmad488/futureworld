#007 COP Process and Global Climate Governance

Climate Intelligence must explain how global negotiations influence national policy, finance, reporting and public expectations.

Global Standard PrincipleCOPUNFCCCGovernance
Governance pathwayNegotiation to implementation
ScienceEvidence from climate research.
NegotiationCOP decisions and global political signals.
National actionNDCs, laws and sector plans.
FinanceSupport for mitigation and adaptation.
DeliveryProjects, reporting and public accountability.

Core rule

Explain COP outcomes in terms of what they change for policy, finance, reporting and field implementation.

FutureWorld application

FutureWorld can turn complex COP language into simple public briefings that show why negotiations matter for communities, forests, water, disaster risk and resilience.

Editorial checklist

  • What was decided or discussed?
  • Which country or sector is affected?
  • Does it influence finance, reporting or implementation?
  • What should local institutions understand?

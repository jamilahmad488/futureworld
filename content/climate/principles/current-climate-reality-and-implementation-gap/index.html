Understanding the gap between knowledge and action

The climate challenge is not only scientific awareness. It is also the gap between known risks and the speed, scale and quality of implementation.

FutureWorld uses this principle to explain why reports, visuals, finance, governance, capacity building and field-level action must be connected.

FutureWorld Climate Intelligence Principle

#014 Disaster Risk Reduction and Early Warning

Climate Intelligence must convert risk knowledge into preparedness, warning, response capacity and reduced losses.

Action PrincipleDRREarly WarningPreparedness
Warning chainKnowledge to action
Risk knowledgeHazard, exposure and vulnerability.
MonitoringWeather, water and impact signals.
WarningClear message and trusted channels.
ResponsePrepared people and institutions.
LearningReview, improve and train again.

Core rule

A warning is only useful when people understand it, trust it and know what action to take.

FutureWorld application

Use this principle for flood, heat, drought, landslide, storm and wildfire preparedness explainers.

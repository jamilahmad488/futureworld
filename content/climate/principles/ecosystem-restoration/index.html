Overview

Restoration is climate infrastructure

Healthy ecosystems reduce climate risk by improving water regulation, soil stability, biodiversity, carbon storage and local resilience.

01ProtectReduce grazing, cutting, burning and disturbance in recovery areas.
02RegenerateSupport natural seedlings, rootstocks and native vegetation.
03RestoreUse planting where natural recovery is weak.
04MonitorTrack vegetation, survival, soil, water and community protection.

FutureWorld treats ecosystem restoration as a strategic bridge between climate action, forestry, watershed management and community participation.

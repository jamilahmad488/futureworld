Implementation, Scaling and Long-Term Stewardship

Move from one-time projects to durable institutions, financing, maintenance, monitoring and community stewardship.

Overview

Climate action must survive beyond the project cycle

Long-term stewardship requires institutions, community ownership, finance, maintenance, monitoring, reporting and learning systems.

FutureWorld uses this principle to focus climate action on durable outcomes rather than short-term activity completion.

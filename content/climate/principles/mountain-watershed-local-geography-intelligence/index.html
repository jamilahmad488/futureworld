FutureWorld Climate Intelligence Principle

#013 Mountain, Watershed and Local Geography Intelligence

Climate action must understand local terrain, water systems, slopes, settlements and mountain vulnerability.

Action PrincipleMountainsWatershedsLocal Intelligence
Local geography lensTerrain changes risk
ElevationTemperature, snow and exposure.
SlopeErosion, landslide and runoff risk.
WaterSprings, streams and recharge.
Land coverForest, rangeland and degradation.
CommunityAccess, livelihoods and preparedness.

Core rule

Never explain climate risk without local geography. Terrain, water, access and land cover shape the real impact.

FutureWorld application

This principle supports field mapping, ANR planning, watershed work, village-level risk briefs and mountain climate education.

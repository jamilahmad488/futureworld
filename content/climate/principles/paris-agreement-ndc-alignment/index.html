FutureWorld Climate Intelligence Principle

#006 Paris Agreement and NDC Alignment

Climate content should connect national and local action with the Paris Agreement, national commitments and long-term climate-resilient development.

Global Standard PrincipleParis AgreementNDCsAccountability
Alignment chainGlobal goal to local delivery
Paris goalLimit warming and strengthen adaptation.
NDCNational climate commitments and priorities.
PolicyLaws, strategies and sector plans.
ProjectMitigation, adaptation and resilience actions.
EvidenceMonitoring, reporting and learning.

Core rule

Every major climate intervention should be explainable in relation to national commitments and the Paris Agreement framework.

FutureWorld application

FutureWorld pages should show how a project, report or local climate story supports mitigation, adaptation, transparency or resilience commitments.

Editorial checklist

  • Which part of national climate planning is relevant?
  • Is the action mitigation, adaptation or both?
  • What evidence can be used for reporting?
  • How does the work help communities and ecosystems?

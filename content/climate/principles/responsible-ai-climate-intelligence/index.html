FutureWorld Climate Intelligence Principle

#015 Responsible AI for Climate Intelligence

AI should strengthen climate understanding, but it must remain transparent, source-aware, human-guided and locally responsible.

Future-Ready PrincipleResponsible AISource-AwareHuman Oversight
AI governance chainUseful intelligence with safeguards
QuestionDefine the climate decision need.
DataUse relevant, documented sources.
AI analysisSummarize, classify and visualize.
ReviewHuman checking and local context.
ActionClear outputs for learning or planning.

Core rule

AI should support climate judgement, not replace scientific evidence, field knowledge or accountable decision-making.

FutureWorld application

Use this principle for AI dashboards, report summaries, map interpretation, early-warning explainers and public learning tools.

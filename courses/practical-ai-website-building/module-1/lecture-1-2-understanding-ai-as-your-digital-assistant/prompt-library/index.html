Prompt Library

Lecture 1.2 copyable prompts

1. Understand AI
Act as a beginner-friendly AI teacher. Explain what AI can and cannot do for a complete beginner who wants to build websites, create content, and learn digital skills. Use simple examples.
2. Turn AI into my assistant
Act as my digital learning assistant. I am a beginner. Ask me questions about my project idea, then guide me step by step on how AI can help me research, plan, write, design, build, and improve my project.
3. Improve my prompt
Act as a prompt improvement coach. I will give you a simple prompt. Improve it by adding role, task, context, output format, and constraints. Explain why the improved prompt is better.
4. AI safety and verification
Act as a responsible AI guide. Teach me how to verify AI answers, avoid mistakes, check facts, and use AI safely for learning and website building.
5. Explain any software tool
Explain [tool name] to me like I am a complete beginner. Tell me what it does, why I need it, when I will use it, and what mistakes beginners should avoid.
6. Compare two tools
Compare [tool A] and [tool B] for a beginner website-building student. Explain the difference in simple language and tell me when to use each one.
7. Installation guide
Act as my beginner computer setup assistant. Give me step-by-step instructions to install [software name] on Windows 10 or Windows 11. Include download link guidance, setup steps, and common mistakes to avoid.
8. Tool usage guide
Act as a patient digital skills teacher. Teach me how to use [tool name] for [specific task]. Give me simple steps, explain each step, and tell me how to confirm that I did it correctly.
9. Troubleshooting guide
I am a beginner and I am facing this problem: [describe problem]. Ask me for any missing details, then guide me step by step to troubleshoot and fix it safely.
10. Prompt formula practice
Teach me the prompt formula Role + Task + Context + Output Format + Constraints. Then help me rewrite this weak prompt into a strong prompt: [paste weak prompt].
11. Learning roadmap
I am a beginner and I want to build a digital platform about [write your topic]. Create a simple learning roadmap for me. Break the journey into small steps and tell me what practical task I should complete today.
12. Ask better questions
Act as a question improvement coach. I will give you a question I want to ask AI. Improve my question so that AI gives me a clearer, more practical and more accurate answer. Explain what you improved.

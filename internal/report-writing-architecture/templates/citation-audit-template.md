## 1. Citation Audit Standard

A citation is acceptable only when:

1. the source exists and is accessible;
2. the source is reputable for the claim being made;
3. the source directly supports the exact claim;
4. the date is suitable for the topic;
5. the source is not misrepresented;
6. the reference details are complete;
7. the citation is placed close to the claim it supports.

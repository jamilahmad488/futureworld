Responsible AI and publishing

Verify before publishing

Check AI-generated facts, links, numbers and claims before placing them on a public website or social media account.

Protect privacy

Do not publish private laptop paths, passwords, personal contact details, confidential documents or sensitive information.

Respect sources

Use your own words, cite sources where required and avoid copying protected material.

Keep human accountability

AI is an assistant. The learner remains responsible for the final publication.

Course Promise

From beginner confusion to a working digital publishing workflow

This course helps you move from "I do not know where to start" to "I can create, publish and manage a simple website with AI support." You do not need previous coding experience.

AI is treated as a support tool, not a replacement for human judgment. Learners are encouraged to review AI-generated content, check facts, improve clarity and publish responsibly.

Learning Outcomes

What you will learn

  • Set up tools and accounts for AI-supported website and content work.
  • Understand GitHub repositories, local folders, Git Bash, commits, pull and push.
  • Build and publish a static website using GitHub Pages and prepare a custom domain.
  • Create articles, website pages, prompts, video pages and social media posts with AI support.
  • Use Google Search Console, sitemap, robots.txt and Google Analytics for responsible monitoring.
  • Build a simple content operations system with an editorial calendar, media library and approval checklist.

Audience

Who this course is for

  • Students learning AI and website publishing.
  • Teachers and trainers creating educational content online.
  • Writers, researchers and creators organizing public knowledge.
  • Small business owners who want to understand digital content.
  • Public-interest teams building a simple online platform.
  • Beginners who want responsible AI and web publishing skills.

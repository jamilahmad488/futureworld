Responsible Use

AI, privacy and public publishing ethics

Verify facts

Check AI-generated claims, links, numbers and names before publishing.

Protect privacy

Do not publish private file paths, passwords, staff details or confidential data.

Respect sources

Use lawful media, avoid copying protected material and cite important sources.

Use analytics carefully

Measure traffic responsibly and provide privacy notices where needed.

Avoid misinformation

Verify high-impact topics from reliable sources before sharing publicly.

Stay accountable

AI assists, but the human learner remains responsible for the final publication.

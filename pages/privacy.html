Privacy & Cookie Policy

FutureWorld Intelligence is an educational, research and public-interest platform focused on climate, artificial intelligence, geopolitics, energy and future systems.

Information We Measure

We use optional analytics cookies only when a visitor accepts them. These help us understand general website traffic, page views, engagement and content performance.

What We Do Not Do

We do not sell visitor data. We do not use advertising cookies. We do not collect precise location data through the website. We do not use cookies for political, commercial or manipulative profiling.

Cookie Choices

Visitors may accept or decline analytics cookies. Cookie preferences can be reset through the “Cookie settings” button on the website.

Third-Party Analytics

If accepted, the website may load Google Analytics to help measure traffic and improve educational content. Analytics data is used in aggregate for platform improvement.
